ELEPHANT The elephant is a massive creature with a long trunk and large ears. Its gray skin is thick and wrinkled, protecting it from the sun. Elephants live in close-knit families called herds and are highly intelligent animals. They consume variety of vegetation such as grass, leaves, and fruits. Found in diverse habitats like forests and grasslands, elephants play a crucial role in shaping their ecosystems. However, they are threatened by habitat loss and illegal hunting for their tusks.
